## 1. Indexation

- Is the website indexable?
- Are important pages blocked by robots.txt?
- Are noindex tags used correctly?
- Is the XML sitemap available?
- Is the sitemap submitted in Google Search Console?
- Are canonical tags implemented correctly?

## 2. Crawlability

- Are there broken internal links?
- Are there redirect chains?
- Are important pages easy to reach internally?
- Are navigation links crawlable?
- Are important pages included in the sitemap?

## 3. On-page SEO

- Are title tags unique and descriptive?
- Are meta descriptions useful?
- Is there one clear H1 per page?
- Are headings structured logically?
- Are images using descriptive alt text where relevant?

## 4. Local SEO

- Is the business name, address and phone number consistent?
- Is there a clear contact page?
- Is LocalBusiness schema implemented?
- Are services described clearly?
- Are location pages useful and not duplicated?

## 5. Structured data

- Is structured data valid?
- Is Organization schema present?
- Is LocalBusiness schema present where relevant?
- Is FAQ schema used only where appropriate?
- Are schema examples tested before publishing?

## 6. Performance

- Are images compressed?
- Are modern image formats used?
- Is unused JavaScript reduced?
- Is unused CSS reduced?
- Are Core Web Vitals checked?
- Is server response time acceptable?

## 7. Content quality

- Does each important page have a clear purpose?
- Are services explained clearly?
- Is outdated content updated?
- Are common customer questions answered?
- Are pages helpful for real users?
