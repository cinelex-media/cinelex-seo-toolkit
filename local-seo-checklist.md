# Local SEO Checklist

A practical checklist for local businesses, agencies and freelancers.

## Google Business Profile

- Use the correct business name
- Add the main business category
- Add secondary categories where relevant
- Add opening hours
- Add services
- Add photos
- Keep contact information consistent

## Website

- Add name, address and phone number clearly
- Create a dedicated contact page
- Add service area information where relevant
- Add location-specific landing pages where useful
- Use LocalBusiness structured data

## Reviews

- Ask satisfied customers for reviews
- Respond to reviews professionally
- Monitor new reviews regularly

## Local citations

- Keep business information consistent
- Check important directories
- Remove duplicate listings where possible

## Content

- Explain services clearly
- Add local references where useful
- Answer common customer questions
- Keep important pages updated
